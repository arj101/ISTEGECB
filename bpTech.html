Bhaskaranpillai Technologies

Bhaskaranpillai Technologies is an online fortnightly quiz series conducted by ISTE GECBH to engage student members within the chapter. More than just a quiz, it allows members to interact in a productive sphere, discuss technical issues and positively debate answers. With interesting trivia and unconventional questions, it is always an instant hit with students, nurturing inquisitiveness and non-linear thinking in the process.

ABOUT US

Established in the year 2013, the ISTE Student Chapter of Government Engineering College, Barton Hill is dedicated to fulfilling the objectives set by the leading national professional non profit Society; the overall development of the technical education system.
The student-run organisation aims to equip themselves to enrich their know-how, apply their knowledge and become leaders in academia. Within a short span of time, the chapter redefined frontiers of technical education developing novel programmes and ensuring incessant academic involvement, becoming the largest technical community by membership strength on campus.
